Our Beacon - Founder, AICT

M. Qamar Joya, an MBA graduate from International Islamic University Islamabad (IIUI), is a visionary entrepreneur whose journey began in 2010 with the founding of AYAN Montessori Institute. His dedication and foresight have since transformed it into Pakistan's leading teachers' training institute, creating a massive alumni network that has influenced major school systems across the nation. Not one to rest on his laurels, Mr. Joya extended his ambitious vision to the corporate world by founding AICT - AYAN Institute of Corporate Training. Through AICT, he aims to elevate Pakistan's workforce, polishing their skills to work smarter and harder, thereby contributing not only to the success of their organizations but also to the collective betterment of the nation. Mr. Joya's remarkable journey is a powerful testament to the transformative impact of relentless hard work, strategic vision, and an unwavering commitment to both educational and professional excellence.
